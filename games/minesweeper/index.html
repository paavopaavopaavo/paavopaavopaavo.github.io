Minesweeper

🚩 10/10 | ⏱️ -.-

How to play

  • Numbers on the grid indicate nearby mines.
  • Right-click to flag suspected mines.
  • Left-click to reveal safe squares.
  • Click a mine to lose.
  • Clear all non-mined squares to win.
  • Use the hint button to reveal a safe square.
